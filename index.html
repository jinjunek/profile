Profile

June is a Project Cost Manager in Mitchell McDermott. A graduate of Limerick Institute of Technology (LIT) where she completed BSc in Quantity Surveying and completed Diploma in Quantity Surveying in Tunku Abdul Rahman University College (TAR UC), Malaysia.

June began her career as a Trainee QS in 2015 with a QS consultancy firm in Malaysia for 4 months and then worked as an Assistant Cost Manager with AECOM in Ireland as part of work placement with her undergraduate degree. She returned to AECOM as a Graduate Quantity Surveyor after college. In May 2018 June became a member of the Mitchell McDermott.

June has been working on large scale new build offices, large fit-outs, residential, hotels, car showrooms, student accommodation and nursing home projects in recent years in Irish Construction industry.
